Silicon Wisdom Manifesto

Discover our vision for the future of human-machine collaboration and cognitive enhancement.

At AgentPsy, we believe that true intelligence does not lie in the perfection of individual entities, but rather in reliable cognitive systems constructed through tools, collaboration, and standards. Our Silicon Wisdom Manifesto outlines a comprehensive approach to human-AI collaboration that emphasizes:

  • Recognition of inherent cognitive limitations in both humans and AI systems
  • The power of tool externalization to overcome cognitive boundaries
  • The importance of collective intelligence through multi-agent collaboration
  • Personalized agent configuration based on rigorous personality and psychological assessments
  • Optimized team composition for enhanced collaborative efficiency and consensus achievement

Our service involves comprehensive, systematic, and detailed evaluation of large models, including personality assessment and cognitive stability assessment, covering various stress scenarios, contextual interference, cognitive trap interference, emotional stress, temperature parameter adjustment, and personality maintenance capability assessment under various personality role settings.
