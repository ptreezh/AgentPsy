Comprehensive Assessment Systems

Scientifically rigorous evaluation of AI agent personality, cognitive stability, and logical robustness

Our assessment systems are built on established psychological theories and adapted for AI evaluation to provide standardized, repeatable testing for large language models and AI agents.

Big Five Personality Assessment

Based on the IPIP-FFM-50 international standard, this assessment evaluates AI personality traits across five dimensions: Extraversion, Agreeableness, Conscientiousness, Neuroticism, and Openness.

  • Scientifically validated psychological framework
  • Standardized 50-item assessment
  • Detailed personality profiling
Learn More

Cognitive Stability Assessment

Adapted from the DASS-42 scale, this assessment evaluates emotional stability and stress response in AI systems, including cognitive bias detection and cognitive dissonance assessment.

  • DASS-42 scale adaptation for AI
  • Comprehensive stability evaluation
  • Risk identification and mitigation
Learn More

Cognitive Trap Assessment

This assessment tests logical thinking and critical reasoning through classic paradoxes, circular reasoning, semantic fallacies, and program traps to evaluate robustness.

  • Logic paradox and fallacy testing
  • Robustness andinterference resistance evaluation
  • Security vulnerability detection
