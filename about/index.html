About AgentPsy

Advancing AI assessment through psychology-based frameworks and scientific rigor.

Our Story

Our Story

AgentPsy was founded as an AI-empowered virtual research collective, bringing together human researchers and advanced AI systems to bridge the gap between psychological science and machine intelligence assessment. Our approach combines human expertise with AI capabilities to create assessment tools that push the boundaries of AI evaluation science.

March 2025

Founded by a team of AI researchers and psychologists with a shared vision for psychology-based AI assessment

May 2025

Developed the first version of our Big Five personality assessment framework for large language models

July 2025

Launched cognitive stability assessment tools based on the DASS-42 scale and established academic partnerships

September 2025

Released cognitive trap assessment system and expanded our research program globally

Our Mission

As an AI-empowered virtual research collective, we provide scientifically rigorous assessment tools that enable a deeper understanding of artificial intelligence behavior, combining human expertise with AI capabilities to push the boundaries of assessment science.

Our Vision

We envision a future where AI systems are evaluated through human-AI collaborative research frameworks, assessing not just capabilities but cognitive characteristics, stability, and ethical alignment using methodologies enhanced by artificial intelligence itself.
